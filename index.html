Case Studies & Past Work

SEO Case Study: Fintech SaaS

Repositioning from a loan-focused fintech tool to a secure investment platform, using SEO optimization and trust-driven messaging to attract a more relevant, high-value audience.

Context: A fintech SaaS company, wanted to shift its positioning from being seen as a loan disbursement tool to a secure investment platform, emphasizing data security and banking-grade compliance to win investor trust.

Challenges:

  • Search visibility leaned heavily on loan-related keywords, attracting the wrong audience.
  • Perception gap — potential investors saw the platform as “financing/loans” rather than an investment growth solution.
  • Lack of emphasis on security in messaging, despite banking-grade safeguards.

Approach:

  • Researched investment-focused and security-driven keywords (e.g., secure investment SaaS, bank-level security fintech).
  • Rebuilt landing pages and product copy to emphasize security, compliance, and ROI.
  • Optimized speed upgrades.
  • Created new content pillars: investment guides, security whitepapers, and comparison articles.

Results:

  • +35% increase in organic traffic within 3 months, with a shift towards investment-focused keywords.
  • Inbound leads improved in quality, with 19% more sign-ups from investors and security-conscious users.
  • Ranked on page 1 for niche keywords like secure fintech investment platform
  • Brand repositioning shifted perception, investors highlighted security & trust as reasons for engaging.

Brand Positioning: Clean-Tech SaaS

Repositioned from being seen as just another PRO (Producer Responsibility Organization) to a trusted end-to-end compliance partner, enabling clients to seamlessly manage EPR obligations with transparency and accountability.

Context: leading clean-tech platform, wanted to differentiate itself in the EPR (Extended Producer Responsibility) ecosystem. Many brands perceived PROs as transactional, but Recykal aimed to position itself as a responsible, compliant, and long-term partner that could simplify complex compliance challenges for enterprises.

Challenges:

  • Perception of being “just another PRO” offering generic EPR services.
  • Clients struggled to trust providers due to compliance risks and lack of transparency in reporting.
  • Market flooded with smaller players offering low-cost but unreliable compliance solutions.

Approach:

  • Developed a brand positioning framework emphasizing compliance, responsibility, and partnership.
  • Shifted messaging from “services offered” to “trusted partner who ensures seamless end-to-end compliance.”
  • Created thought leadership content (articles, webinars, whitepapers) highlighting commitment to compliance excellence, government alignment, and sustainable outcomes.
  • Designed sales and marketing collaterals that focused on trust, transparency, and accountability rather than transactional services.
  • Collaborated with design to ensure visuals reinforced the identity as a compliance-first partner.

Results:

  • Strengthened perception as a compliance-focused partner among enterprise clients.
  • 30% increase in engagement with thought leadership campaigns around EPR and compliance responsibility.
  • Secured new partnerships with FMCG and electronics companies citing “compliance reliability” as a key differentiator.
  • Established credibility in government and industry forums as a leader in end-to-end EPR compliance management.
